Breaking down misinformation - Different types of fake news

To better understand the misinformation ecosystem, we should break it down in different categories. Many organizations already attempted that, and according to FirstDraftNews it's divided in 7 groups:

Satire or parody

Misinformation created for satire or parody purposes, are those news an individual creates to perhaps prank their peers. These information was not created to directly harm someone, but should be of course avoided as people can have different interpretations.

False connection

False connections are very common, this is when for instance a photo is either outdated or as nothing to do with the description/content.

Imposter content

This is when trustful sources are impersonated to seem more credible. For example saying that this article is based on study x or that this article developed by credible individuals.

Manipulated content

Fabricated content

Finally, there is the fully fabricated and designed content, which only has the objective to cause harm and chaos.
